About Us

任童動畫工作室致力於以動畫重塑香港曾引以為傲的動作片。團隊由具電影與廣告實戰經驗的專業動畫師及動作指導組成，成員們參與製作的作品包括
《明日戰記》、《尋秦記》、《守闕者》、《九龍城寨之圍城》、《觸電》、Netflix 《芭比》、Dreamworks 《蓋比的娃娃屋大電影》等等
我們結合電影敘事與先進製作技術，包括3D掃描、動作與面部捕捉，以及電影級合成等流程
我們相信動畫應該有靈魂、有生命，而非流於模板與公式
每一個鏡頭，都是對香港動作美學的延續，並以更大的視野，走向國際舞台
At Wild Don Animation Studio, we don’t just produce animation. We bring Hong Kong action to life in digital form
Our team is composed of professional animators and action directors with hands-on experience in film and advertising
Collectively, our members have contributed to productions including Warriors of Future, Back the Past, The Trier of Fact, Twilight of the Warriors: Walled In, Good Game, Netflix’s Barbie, and DreamWorks’ Gabby’s Dollhouse: The Movie, among others
We combine cinematic storytelling with advanced production techniques, including 3D scanning, motion and facial capturing, cinematic compositing, etc. Our pipeline is built on Blender as our core engine, with AI integrated as a precision assist tool to enhance efficiency while maintaining full creative control
We believe animation should feel alive, not generic
Every frame we craft carries attitude, movement, and the spirit of Hong Kong action cinema, reimagined for a global audience
